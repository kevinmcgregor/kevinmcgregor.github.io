About Kevin


I’m currently a PhD candidate in Biostatistics in McGill University’s Department of Epidemiology, Biostatistics, and Occupational Health. My co-supervisors are Celia Greenwood and Aurélie Labbe.

My research interests include:

  • Statistical genetics
  • Analysis of high-dimensional data
  • Network analysis in microbiome data
  • Bayesian statistical methods
  • Statistical analysis of DNA methylation data
